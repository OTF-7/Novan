Our source & process
Purity

Nine stages between source and seal.

Nature does most of the work — our role is to protect it. We test, filter and seal with obsessive care, then verify every batch in our own laboratory.

Sourced

Drawn from a protected deep aquifer beneath the highlands.

Filtered

Micro and reverse-osmosis filtration remove the finest impurities.

Sterilised

UV and ozone treatment ensure water that is biologically pure.

Sealed & tested

Bottled on a sealed line and verified batch-by-batch in our lab.

Responsibility

Lighter on the land we love.

The mountains gave us this water. Protecting them is not a campaign — it is the condition of everything we make.

Lightweight bottles. Redesigned with less plastic in every cap and body.

100% recyclable PET. Every bottle is designed to return, not to waste.

Water stewardship. We draw only what the aquifer can naturally replenish.
